# Task 2: AI Content Marketing using UGC Ads

This repository contains the complete AI-generated User-Generated Content (UGC) Ad Pack for **EatClub Daily Meal Boxes**. The assets include the multi-character voiceover script, structural prompt logics, hooks, ad copy variations, and the final video output generated via Adobe Firefly.

## 📦 Project Overview: "EatClub Daily Meal Boxes"
* **Target Audience:** College students living in hostels or PGs facing heavy study schedules, zero kitchen access, and uninspiring mess food.
* **Core Objective:** Design and deploy a highly persuasive UGC video ad campaign. The strategy leverages psychological hooks, authentic student pain points, and platform-specific formatting (Instagram, YouTube Shorts) to convert tired students into long-term meal box subscribers.

---

## 🛠️ System Prompt & Persona Initialization

To establish brand consistency and direct-response copywriting precision before generating the content framework, the following persona guidelines were set:

Persona: You are a Direct-Response Copywriter, Behavioral Psychologist, and Viral UGC Content Strategist.
Task: Draft a scroll-stopping UGC video script pack for "EatClub Daily Meal Boxes" targeting tired hostel students.
Tone & Voice: Highly relatable, slightly chaotic but empathetic, authentic, and fast-paced.

Write a 30-second multi-character voiceover script for an EatClub Daily Meal Box UGC advertisement. 
1. The opening must capture the voice of an exhausted female student complaining about studying for 10 hours straight while eating nothing but instant noodles.
2. Script a clean voice transition introducing EatClub as the healthy, fresh, local alternative designed to maintain cognitive focus.
3. Introduce a secondary voice perspective: A male student highlighting the specific pain points of living in a hostel or PG with zero kitchen access and eating greasy, brain-fog-inducing mess food.
4. Conclude with a high-urgency call to action linking nutrition directly to saving academic grades.
